## Research Project Context

**Project Title:** Longitudinal Evaluation of Mental Model Interventions in Task Performance and Trust Calibration  
**Goal:**  
To conduct long-term user studies that evaluate the effects of specific mental model-enhancing interventions on user task performance, error recovery, trust, and reliance over extended interaction periods.  
**Contribution:**  
This project provides empirical evidence regarding the durability and persistent impact of mental model improvements, highlighting the relationship between mental model interventions and real-world human-robot team effectiveness and safety over time.
