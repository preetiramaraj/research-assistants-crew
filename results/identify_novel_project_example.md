### Project 5: Longitudinal Evaluation of Mental Model Interventions in Task Performance and Trust Calibration

**Goal:**  
To conduct long-term user studies that evaluate the effects of specific mental model-enhancing interventions (from previous projects) on user task performance, error recovery, trust, and reliance over extended interaction periods.

**Contribution:**  
This project will provide empirical evidence regarding the durability and persistent impact of mental model improvements, highlighting the relationship between mental model interventions and real-world human-robot team effectiveness and safety over time—a topic rarely studied systematically in the existing HRI literature.
